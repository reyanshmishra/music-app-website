Awesome Features

We've got it all.

Beautiful design with cool animations/

Specially designed looking after User experience and their need. Flawless and eye catchy animations.

Smart Equalizer/

Set equalizer for your song, genres, playlist, albums, artist and this its of 12 band eqaulizer with more than 10 presets.

Awesome features/

Scrobbling, saving tracks position, limiting music folders, Mp3 tagging, beatiful inbuild FileExplorer, BlackLists for 'albums, artist, songs' and more

/Smart Queue

Now access your music queue anywhere in the app its no need to shuffle around. Sort your queue with mostly played, favorites name and remove items from the queue by just swipping and undo it.

/Live Lyrics

Displays the live lyrics of playing songs, lyrics can be saved or emmbedded in the mp3 file. (Though its little complicated for us to achieve that greatness Please bear with us.)

/Easy to user

Wit the minimilistic design and less cluttered UI its super easy to use, with three screen widgets you are on click away from your favorites song.
